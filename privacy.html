Welcome to DalJord Creations. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data when you play Idle Farm Tycoon.

02

Information Collection

  • Device information: model, OS version, language, IP address, and crash logs.
  • Advertising identifiers: mobile advertising ID (AAID/IDFA).
  • Gameplay analytics: session length, progress milestones, feature usage.
  • In‑app purchase data: receipt confirmations from Apple App Store and Google Play.

We do not require user login or account creation to play.

03

Data Usage

  • Operate and improve gameplay features.
  • Measure performance, stability, and crashes.
  • Deliver ads and measure ad performance.
  • Respond to support requests.
04

Third‑Party Services

We use third‑party services that may collect data under their own policies:

  • Unity Ads (advertising) — Policy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — Policy
  • Firebase Analytics (analytics) — Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting) — Policy

In‑app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not store payment card details.

05

Data Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers listed above or when required by law.

06

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have done so, we will delete it promptly.

07

Data Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect information. No system is completely secure, but we continuously work to protect data from unauthorized access.
